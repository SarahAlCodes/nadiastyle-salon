All About Nadia

Nadia is a Florida Board Certified stylist who graduated from Kaizen Beauty Academy where she gained her mastery in makeup artistry, hair cutting, coloring, and styling. She is a Master Colorist with expertise in balayages, highlights and bringing any hair to life! Nadia is constantly learning the newest techniques to create the best in hair and makeup styles, to make every client feel as beautiful inside and out!

Nadia has always had a passion for hair and makeup for as little as she could remember! Her clients describe her hands as magic, for the way she brings their hair back to life and how fast their hair grows after getting haircuts. Since Nadia began her career as a Cosmetologist, seeing her clients positive reactions to their hair and makeup always brought her the most joy. Her love for making every woman feel beautiful and building connections with her loyal clients, gave birth to Nadia's Style Beauty Salon.
